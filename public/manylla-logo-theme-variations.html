Implementation Notes

Light Mode: Original subtle gradient with standard red accent. Best for professional contexts and maximum readability.

Dark Mode: Lighter gradient colors for contrast against dark background. Brighter red for better visibility.

Manylla Mode: Deeper, richer browns to complement the manila envelope background. Darker red for stronger presence.

Transitions: When switching themes, consider a 0.3s transition on colors for smooth visual feedback.
