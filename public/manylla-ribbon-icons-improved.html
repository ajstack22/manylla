Design Improvements

Based on research of modern minimalist principles:

  • Used SVG for perfect scalability and crisp rendering at all sizes
  • Applied geometric precision with consistent stroke weights and proportions
  • Incorporated negative space effectively for visual breathing room
  • Maintained single-color approach for maximum versatility
  • Ensured icons work at extreme sizes (16px favicon to 512px app store)
  • Created visual metaphors that connect to manila envelope string-tie closures
  • Balanced abstract representation with recognizable forms

Recommended: The Geometric Y (Icon #1) successfully merges the letter "Y" with a string-tie closure visual, creating a memorable mark that works across all applications while maintaining brand connection to the typographic logo.
