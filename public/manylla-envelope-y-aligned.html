Y Aligned to Flap Line

The "y" is positioned so its junction point (where the arms meet the stem) sits exactly at the envelope's flap line

Primary Concept
The red "y" crossing point aligns perfectly with the flap line, creating a visual connection between the letter form and the envelope closure mechanism

Design rationale: By aligning the Y's junction with the flap line, the letter becomes an integral part of the envelope's structure, reinforcing the string-tie closure metaphor while maintaining brand recognition.
